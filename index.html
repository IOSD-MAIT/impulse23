2022

With IMPULSE'22, we successfully organized the third fest of our tech-fest series, "IMPULSE". This was the first offline tech fest organized in the college offline after COVID 19. With a plethora of events from our iconic HackMAIT 3.0 which was a 36hr hackathon, Web Wizards (Web Designing competition), Sudo Solve(Competitive Coding) to some gaming events like BGMI and HourGlass(Chess).

2019

With IMPULSE'19, we successfully organized the second version of our tech-fest series, "IMPULSE". From Algorythm, the event, especially for the coding geeks to gaming where PubG and CS: Go players were slashing the enemies to get to the finals. DevDash, the first-ever blind web development event in the history of MAIT was itself a unique experience for the participants and volunteers as well, Explora-the workshop where students learned a lot about competitive coding and interview preparation. But the most special event was HackMAIT 2.0, a 36-hours hackathon, where the winners were awarded prizes worth 1 lac.

2018

Impulse' 18 was the first tech fest of IOSD-MAIT and it was a great success. With crowd engaging events such as PIXEL: UI/UX designing event and CodeSaga: a competitive coding event to an enthusiastic game development workshop. With Impulse' 18 we also organized the first version of HackMAIT, a 36-hour hackathon, and the winners were awarded prizes worth 1 lac.
