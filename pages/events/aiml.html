Neural Network Nexus

AI/ML

IOSD-MAIT welcomes you to Neural Network Nexus to showcase your skills in Machine Learning. Here you will be given a challenge to solve real world problem in a limited time using Machine Learning. You'll build a recommender system which will have potential to solve real business problems.

So get your neural network ready to predict the future!

Rules

General Guidelines

  • Registration is to be done by Team Leader in case a team of 2 members are participating otherwise individual participant details are required.
  • Team size can be of maximum 2 members.
  • This is a one day event. Event will start at 11AM and teams/participants can continue to code till 4PM in the college campus, but the submissions can be accepted till 8PM.

Assessment Guidelines

  • Assessment will be based on the accuracy of the model, how clean your code is, how EDA is performed and most importantly how your model reacts to raw data given by a user directly.
  • Dataset for the problem statement will be shared via Google Drive link to all the teams/participants on the event day.
  • Submissions will be accepted only through GitHub repository of your project, further details on submission will be instructed on the event day.
  • Results will be evaluated till 10PM and the winning team/participant will be informed and will be invited to MAIT Campus on the second day for the prize distribution and the event will be concluded.

General Rules

  • Inter-college teams/participants are allowed
  • It's mandatory for each participant to carry a College ID card for the competition.
  • All decisions in matters of eligibility, authenticity & marking made by the judges shall be final and binding.
  • Any attempts at malpractice and/or plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
  • All the event-specific rules and guidelines will be conveyed before the event.
  • Organizers reserve the right to change, modify or postpone any aspect of the event without prior notice. In case of any dispute, the decision of the organizers shall be final and binding
  • Teams/participants failing to adhere to the deadlines will be disqualified
  • All the participants have to reach the venue 15 min before the time.
  • Each round will be an eliminatory round
