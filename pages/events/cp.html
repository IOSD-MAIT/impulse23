Code Crusade

competitive Programming

IOSD MAIT is organizing a coding contest to provide a platform for coding enthusiasts to showcase their skills and team spirit. The contest comprises of two rounds that extensively test participants on their coding knowledge and problem-solving abilities based on data structures and algorithms.

The contest is designed to be a great learning experience for participants, and it provides an opportunity to engage with like-minded individuals who share a passion for coding. Participants will also have the chance to network with industry professionals and gain valuable insights into the world of coding and software development.

The event will consist of 2 rounds:

  • Round 1: First round will be an elimination based round where every participant will participate individually. It consists of 3 elimination stages, passing which participants reach the second round. Each elimination round will have two questions based on data structures and algorithms to be solved in 20 minutes.
  • Round 2: Second round will be an ICPC style team contest where the participants will be grouped into teams of two and each team will have to give the contest on a single computer system. It will be a 1.5 hour contest where teams will solve a set of problems based on data structures and algorithms.
  • Round 3 (Presentation Round): In the final round, each team is required to demonstrate their functional project in front of the pannel.

  • Each round will be an eliminatory round
