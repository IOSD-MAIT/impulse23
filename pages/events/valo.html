Valorant Tournament

IOSD-MAIT inviting players of the video game Valorant to participate in a competitive event where they can compete against other players and prove themselves as the best Valorant player. It is a great opportunity for players who love the game and are looking for a chance to showcase their skills and abilities.

Even if you're a rookie player, you'll get the chance to meet and play with some of the best players in your area. This is a unique opportunity to learn from experienced players and to gain insights into the strategies and tactics they use to play the game at a high level.

Valorant is a team-based shooter game that requires coordination, communication, and individual skill. Playing in a competitive event can be a great way to improve your gameplay by learning from others and testing your own abilities against skilled opponents.

In addition to the competitive aspect, this event is also a chance to meet and connect with other players who share your passion for Valorant. You'll be able to make new friends and connections, all while enjoying the thrill of competition and improving your skills.

So, if you're a Valorant player looking for a challenge and a chance to prove yourself, don't hesitate to join this event. It's an opportunity to meet new people, learn from experienced players, and have a great time doing what you love.

Rules

General Guidelines

  • It is a 5 (+1) player-team tournament. A 5 man-starter roster and up to 1 substitute.
  • There is no restriction on whether the team members are from the same organization/college or not.
  • Valorant anti-cheat software (Vanguard) is kernel-based software that boots even before you with your operating system.
  • If Vanguard detects any suspicious activity, your system will be hardware banned.
  • A moderator would be spectating the matches at all times.

Tournament Guidelines

  • Map Pool: Ascent, Split, Haven, Icebox, Pearl, Lotus & Fracture
  • All maps will be played in the standard competitive 5v5 game mode
  • The tournament will be played as knockout (single elimination) matches, where teams will be eliminated if they lose a match
  • All the first stage matches will be the best of the 1 series (BO1)
  • 4/8 teams will qualify for the second stage which will be conducted offline on Mait campus.
  • All second-stage matches will be best of 3 series (BO3)
  • Losers from the semifinals will play the best of 3 matches to determine third place
  • Final will be the best of 3 series (BO3)

General Rules

  • Inter-college teams are allowed
  • Only the Team Representative/Leader shall fill the form.
  • One player can play from one team only.
  • Minimum 4 players in a team to show up on the match day, or else the other team will get a BYE and play the next round
  • It's mandatory for each participant to carry a College ID card for the tournament.
  • All decisions in matters of eligibility, authenticity & marking made by the judges shall be final and binding.
  • All the event-specific rules and guidelines will be conveyed before the event.
  • Organizers reserve the right to change, modify or postpone any aspect of the event without prior notice. In case of any dispute, the decision of the organizers shall be final and binding
  • Teams failing to adhere to the deadlines will be disqualified
  • All the participants have to reach the venue 15 min before the time.
  • Each round will be an eliminatory round
